Internet of Things

  • We sought out to create a "smart shelf" using the Internet of Things
  • The purpose of the shelf system is to be capable of identifying whether somebody is standing in front of a shelf and if the shelf area is stocked.
  • Thus our project can be broken down into two broader categories: developing our shelf system prototype and creating our website to interface with the sensors.

Recall

  • The Internet of Things (IoT) is a system where we can interact with sensors and everyday objects remotely.
  • We are developing our system to be compatible with the OGC IoT SensorThings API.
